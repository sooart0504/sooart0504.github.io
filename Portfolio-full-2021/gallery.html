Upcoming Project

1. Wandering Within: Creation of Planet Serisse

Upcoming series of project will include 3D modeling in digital space. This project will be the start of bridging the gap between my physical and digital work. The work will consist of abstract shapes and textures that depict a sense of immersion and a certain emotion. Each piece will showcase different affective processes within my daily life such as happiness, joy, sadness, anger, frustration, and everything in between.


2. Project RPG: Gamification of Real-world Behavior

This project will visually augment a gamified everyday behaviors in my daily life. The medium will consist of both the 3D and 2D elements. Similar to how RPG games work, I will portray myself as a game character where each behavioral decision in daily life affects my character as a whole. For example, when I eat an apple, I will gain energy points. This project is a fun way to objectively visualize and reflect on my own behaviors.


3. Planet Unknown: An exploration of the NFT Art scene

This project will explore the NFT art scene by creating a virtual character who makes artworks and sells them in the virtual world. This project will be used as a practice ground for learning and understanding the future NFTs.
